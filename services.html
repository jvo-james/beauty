Before you book

Good to know.

Can I choose studio or home service?

Yes. Some services can be booked as studio appointments or home service, depending on availability.

Will I see available times online?

Yes. The booking page will later show only the dates and time slots that are open.

Do I pay before my booking is confirmed?

Yes. Payment will be handled on the website with Paystack before the booking is saved.

Can prices be updated later?

Yes. Once connected to Firebase, services and prices can be managed from the admin page.
